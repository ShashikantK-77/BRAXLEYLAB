About the company:

Kiran Pharmaceutical Established in the year 2006, is an ambitious company started with a view to launch high quality, research based innovative products in the field of Pediatrics, Orthopaedics and Gynacolgy.

Kiran Pharmaceutical believes that what really matters is not so much success but the way in which it is achieved. Hence, we focus mainly on the quality of our products. All our products are WHO cGMP Certified and are made maintaining the most stringent quality standards.

Our products are formulated, made and tested according to the rigorous criteria of the pharmaceutical industry and sold in India and various countries across the world, Kiran Pharmaceuticals products accompany patients throughout the world for a better management of patient treatments.

We are manufacturers of various dosage forms like tablets, capsules, liquid orals and external preparations such as ointments, creams, lotions and proprietary ayurvedic formulations. Our capabilities and expertise over the years have resulted in continued demand for our patented and proprietary medicines. We have various products ranges like Paediatrics, Gynaecology, Orthopaedics, Dentistry, ENT and Ayurvedic.

Our brands like Lacease Drops and Jodvardhak Oil are the leading brands in their categories. Lacease Drops has been the most trusted for Lactose Intolerance in Paediatrics. It is the leading brand in India in Lactose Intolerance in Paediatrics. Lacease Drops has been treating many for over 2 years.
